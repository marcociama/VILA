How VILA works

Three objects. One photo. Your wallet, forever recoverable.

📷
STEP 01

Photograph your scene

Place three ordinary, unrelated objects together — a marker, a can, a ball. Photograph them. That arrangement is your key.

🧠
STEP 02

ViT encodes the scene

A frozen Vision Transformer processes the image into a 384-dimensional latent vector. No training. No personal data stored.

🔑
STEP 03

Vector becomes key

The vector is passed to standard BIP-32 derivation. Your wallet address is derived. On recovery: same objects → same vector → same wallet.

Security properties

Kerckhoffs compliant. Algorithm fully public. Security entirely in the arrangement.

No dictionary attack

Object arrangements are not in any vocabulary. Knowing the objects does not help enumerate the spatial configuration.

No enumeration

Visual latent space is continuous and infinite (R³⁸⁴). The attack surface is all possible spatial compositions — not a finite word list.

Nothing to steal

The secret is a physical arrangement in the real world. The vault file is encrypted and useless without the matching scene.

Explainable authentication

Attention rollout shows exactly which regions drove the key. You see why you were granted access — no black box.
